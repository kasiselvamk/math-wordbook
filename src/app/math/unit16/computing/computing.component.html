COMPUTING

  1. The Mathematician "Charles Babbage" is considered as the Father of Computer Science. A computer is a device which performs calculations at an unbelievable fast rate.

    The diagramatic or pictorial representation of steps of solving a problem is called a flow-chart.

    Four types of boxes are used in a flow chart.

    Rectangular Box (Calculation)

    Oval shape terminal box (Start and Stop)

    Rhombus Shaped box - Diamond box (Decision box)

    Pentagonal box - Input - Output

  2. A technical term for step by step procedure of solving a problem is called an algorithm.

    BASIC, PASCAL, COBOL, FORTRAN, are some of the programming languages.
