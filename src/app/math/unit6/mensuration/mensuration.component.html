MENSURATION

  1. Perimeter : Sum Of the lengths of the boundaries is called its perimeter.

  2. Area : The magnitude of a plane region is called its area.

  3. Area of a triangle ={{"\\frac{1}{2}"}}\times base\times height

  4. Perimeter of a triangle a + b + c = 2S

  5. S = Semi perimeter ={{"\\frac{a+b+c}{2}"}}

  6. Area of Scalene triangle = \sqrt{{"{s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)}"}}

  7. Altitude of an equilateral triangle = \frac{{"{\\sqrt3}{2}"}}\times a^2

    Area of an equilateral triangle

    Perimeter of an eql. =3a

  8. Area of a Quadrilateral = {{"\\frac{1}{2}"}}\times d \times (h_1+h_2)

  9. Area of a Trapezium = {{"\\frac{1}{2}"}}\times h\times (a+b)

    (a, b are the lengths of the parallel sides)

  10. Area of a parallelogram = base x altitude

  11. Area of rhombus = base\times height\;(or)\;{{"\\frac{1}{2}"}}\times d_1\times d_2

    (when the diagonals are given)

    Perimeter of a rhombus = 4 \times Side

  12. Area of a rectangle =length x breadth

    Perimeter of a rectangle 2\times (1+b)

    Diagonal of a rectangle =\sqrt{{"{l^2+h^2}"}}=d

  13. Area of a Square = Side x Side or

    {{"\\frac{1}{2}"}}\times d^2\; d=diagonal

  14. Perimeter of a Square = 4 x Side

    diagonal of a square =\sqrt {{"{2}"}}\times Side

  15. Area of a Circle = \pi r^2

    Circumference of a Circle = 2\pi r\;or\;\pi d

  16. Area of a Sector of a circle {{"\\frac{\\theta }{360}"}} \pi r^2

    (\theta is angle at the centre)

  17. Perimeter of the Sector = l+ 2r

    Area of the Sector = {{"\\frac{1}{2}"}}lr^2

  18. Area of the Segrnent APB

    = Area of Sector AOBP - Area of AAOB

    \begin{{"{bmatrix}"}}{{"\\frac{\\theta}{360}"}} \pi r^2 - {{"\\frac{1}{2}"}}r^2 \sin \theta \end{{"{bmatrix}"}}

  19. Area of Circular ring = \pi (R^2 - r^2)

  1. Cube :

    a) A Cube is a cuboid whose length, breadth and height are all equal.

    b) Volume of a Cube = Side\times Side\times Side =\;a^3

    Surface area of a Cube = 6\times Side^2 = 6a^2

    Length of the longest diagonal \sqrt 3 \times a

    Area of the four walls of a room 2h (l+b) =\;Perimeter\;of\;the\;floor \times\;height

  1. Cylinder :

    Volume = \pi r^2 h

    Curved Surface Area = 2\pi rh

    Total Surface Area = 2\pi r (h + r)

  1. Cone :

    Volume = {{"\\frac{1}{3}"}} \pi r^2h

    Curved Surface Area = \pi rl

    Slant height l = \sqrt {{"{r^2+h^2}"}}

    Total Surface Area \pi r(l+r)

  1. Sphere :

    Volume = {{"\\frac{4}{3}"}} \pi r^3

    Surface Area = 4\pi r^2

  1. Hemisphere :

    Volume = {{"\\frac{2}{3}"}} \pi r^3

    Total Surface Area = 3 \pi r^2

    Curved Surface Area = 2 \pi r^2

  1. Frustum of a Cone
